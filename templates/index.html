Pixora - Album Sharing App

Pixora is a user-friendly album-sharing web application that allows users to create, upload, and share photo albums with others. Users can also mark albums as their favorites, making it easy to revisit memorable moments.

Key Features:

  • Easy sign-up and login with user authentication
  • Google Drive integration for seamless album storage
  • Create, upload, and share albums with a shareable link
  • Mark albums as favorites to organize cherished moments
  • View and upload photos through a shared link with restricted access
  • Responsive design for both desktop and mobile users
